About Us

CSE club (Club Scientifique de l'ESI) is another student club at the Higher National School of Computer Science in Algiers. It was founded in 2006 by a group of students who were passionate about computer science, high-tech, robotics, design, and technology in general. The club's mission is to provide a platform for students to explore their passions, develop their skills, and network with others in the field
